DC Movies

1.The Dark Knight


Why is the Dark Knight my number one pick?

The Dark Knight became my all-time favorite movie of DC movies because of Heath Ledger and his legendary performance as the joker. He took a complex and crazy character like The Joker and turned him into a chaos agent whose mission was creating as much destruction, and ruin in Gotham as possible, with a goal to create fear doubt and uncertainty among the people of Gotham. The writing was spectacular, the performance from all the leads was immaculate, and the musical score was amazing

Overall I rate The Dark Knight a 10/10
